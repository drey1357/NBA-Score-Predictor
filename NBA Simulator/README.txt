This application uses basic statistics from the 2021-2022 season of the National Basketball Association (NBA) season to predict the outcome of a game between two teams selected by the user.
Users are also permitted to select each team in the matchup randomly.
Statistics were obtained from the NBA's website and can be viewed at the following web addresses:

https://www.nba.com/stats/teams/traditional/?sort=TEAM_NAME&dir=-1&SeasonType=Regular%20Season&Season=2021-22&PerMode=Per48

https://www.nba.com/stats/teams/opponent/?sort=TEAM_NAME&dir=-1&Season=2021-22&SeasonType=Regular%20Season&PerMode=Per48
